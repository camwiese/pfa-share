A new civic destination for San Francisco at the Palace of Fine Arts

A place to enjoy with your kids, family, and friends to experience beauty, wonder, and hope for the future.

In 1915, San Francisco invited the world to visit a similar place.

We hosted the Panama-Pacific International Exposition – a World’s Fair celebrating human achievement and our spirit of possibility.

Its last remaining structure, the Palace of Fine Arts, has been patiently awaiting its next chapter.

Welcome to the Future

Our vision for San Francisco’s new home of progress.

Palace of Fine Arts · 2030

The Palace has been reimagined to once again capture our spirit of innovation and celebrate the best San Francisco has to offer the world.

Grab coffee, wander through dozens of statues honoring history’s greatest innovators, picnic on the lawn, and stay for a fountain show that closes every evening.

Explore over 60 stalls where single-item specialists obsess over one perfect dish, local chefs showcase the Bay Area’s global culinary culture, and food pioneers serve what farming and science have made possible.

Step into a Tomorrow. Explore a clean energy facility powering the world, an ocean station restoring coral reefs, a space station pushing the boundaries of medicine, and a community where today’s innovations are part of everyday life.

Meet the scientists behind the breakthroughs you just walked through, work alongside them in hands-on workshops, and find your own way to help build the future.

Every visit leaves you with a sense of wonder — and a reason to come back.

Help write the next chapter in the story of San Francisco.

We’re assembling a coalition of founders, funders, and builders to bring it to life.
