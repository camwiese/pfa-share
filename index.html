A new civic destination for San Francisco at the Palace of Fine Arts

We’re building a place you can visit to experience beauty, wonder, and hope for the future.

In 1915, the Panama-Pacific International Exposition welcomed 19 million people to San Francisco — a celebration of possibility that put our city on the world stage.

The last building standing from that fair is the Palace of Fine Arts.

We’re assembling a coalition of founders, funders, and builders to write its next chapter.

Welcome to the Future

Our vision for San Francisco’s new home of progress.

Palace of Fine Arts · 2030

The Palace has been reimagined to once again capture our spirit of innovation and celebrate the best San Francisco has to offer the world.

Tour into Tomorrow is an immersive journey through four destinations set in the not-too-distant future — each one inspired by real breakthroughs happening across the Bay Area.

Future Lab is where inspiration becomes action — a hands-on STEM center where kids and adults learn directly from the scientists behind the breakthroughs they just experienced.

At the heart of it all, a beautiful space celebrates the history of the Exposition, with dining experiences showcasing the Bay Area’s global culinary culture and the future of food.

Every detail reimagined, creating one of the most beautiful public spaces in the country — open to all, from morning to night.

Every visit leaves you with a sense of wonder — and a reason to come back.

Join us in writing the next chapter
in the story of San Francisco.
